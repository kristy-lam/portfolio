Kristy's Top 3 Movie Picks

These are the best! Don't miss out!

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

This is the start of an epic journey. You may remember the famous meme about Boromir and Mordor from the movie.

The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers

Being the second leg of the trilogy, it continues to wow audience with grand battle scenes. The acting is underrated.

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

The perfect ending to the story. The only bad thing about the movie is that it's too short! The extended edition is the one to go for.
